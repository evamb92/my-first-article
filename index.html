#LocalQueensBusinessesareboomingduringtheU.SOpen

Eva Mbengue @cunyjschool#5:30pm

On Wednesday 28th August around 11 p.m hundreds of people get out at Mets-Willets Point station in Queens. The reason ? They want to see their favourite athletes like Roger Federer, Rafael Nadal, Serena Williams, Naomi Osaka or Novak Djokovic competing for the U.S. Open title. Like every summer the ‘Grand Slam’ tournament takes place in Billie Jean King National Tennis Center in Flushing Meadows-Corona. And according to the United States Tennis Association (U.S.T.A), the tournament generates more than $750 million per year to New York City.
